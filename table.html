Facts About Virat Kohli

  • Kohli made his international debut in 2008.
  • He has received numerous awards including the ICC ODI Player of the Year.
  • Kohli is the fastest player to score 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, and 11,000 runs in ODIs.
  • As the ODI Cricketer of the Year, Kohli is the youngest player to have won the title. He was named the top run scorer in 2011 after amassing 1381 runs in 34 One-Day Internationals. In 2012, he received this recognition. Virat Kohli broke the record for the most double centuries as a test captain in December 2017. He is the only Indian batsman to score 500-plus runs in a bilateral ODI series.
  • He experienced a cervical spine problem during a test match against South Africa in 2018. After this, the player, who was born in Delhi, became a vegetarian. Delhi played Karnataka in the Ranji Trophy at the Arun Jaitley Stadium in December 2006. At that time, Kohli suffered greatly due to the demise of his father.
